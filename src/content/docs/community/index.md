Since ScorpioBot is community-driven project, we are always looking for ways to make it the best experience for both
users and developers. This page contains some details about how we handle different things.

## Community support
We have a [dedicated Discord server for the ScorpioBot community](https://discord.gg/GvyuDDFeZU). If you have any questions, 
suggestions, or need help with the bot, feel free to join us and ask for help. We are always eager to help you both in setting up
and module development.

## Modules
Currently, there are 3 types of modules for ScorpioBot. These represent the standard which each module meets in terms of maintenance, origin and quality.

### Official modules
Official modules are **maintained by the same people as ScorpioBot Core**.
- They meet high quality standards
- Maintenance and bug fixes are to be expected as soon as possible
- Are always compatible with the latest bot release
- Can be downloaded for free from the [dedicated GitHub repo](#)

While ScorpioBot prioritizes changes and bugfixes in the core, we want to provide a free multi-functional option.

:::tip
While we do plan to have multiple official modules for community to use, you can always release your own versions with different
concepts, and they will be verified as long as they are unique in some way.
:::

### First-party modules
While official modules are still maintained by the project maintainers, they are mostly single-person operation and are not officially supported.
- They try meet high quality standards
- Maintenance and bug fixes depend on developer's availability
- Are not guaranteed to be compatible with the latest release
- Can be both free or paid

These modules can be made by project maintainers and are privately owned work available both for free or paid on one of the [verified marketplaces](/community/marketplaces/).

### Third-party modules
Third-part modules are made by the community and can meet lower standard of quality.

#### Verified third-party modules
Module being verified means that it meets at least basic standards of quality and are safe to execute.
Each verified module is checked for exploits, errors, bugs or other issues.

ScorpioBot team does not guarantee maintenance of the modules but will try their best to ensure each verified community developer is able to properly maintain their work.

## Community developers
Since ScorpioBot is module-based, we take this aspect of the community very seriously. This is to ensure both quality, experience
and security. That's why, we have a dedicated **Community Developers program** which includes multiple processes to sign high quality
modules from trustworthy developers with extra highlight.

### Participating in the program
To join **Community Developer program** you and your resources need to meet the [Developer Guidelines](/community/guidelines/) and submit every public ScorpioBot 
resource you release - both free and paid - for verification, which is handled by **ScorpioBot Maintainers**. This is to ensure, your
module is safe, working properly and meets our quality standards.

:::danger
Verification process which is described below is currently not available since we are still working on implementing it.
The system will be available to users upon the initial release.
:::

### Module verification
Before publicly releasing any ScorpioBot resource you need to submit it for verification. In order to do that, join our [Discord Community](https://discord.gg/GvyuDDFeZU)
and open a ticket using our support system.

#### Verification process
After submitting your resource for verification, we will verify the code, test it and check for possible errors. We may ask some extra
questions and request changes like fixing bugs. You don't need to worry, we also are developers and know that mistakes happen all the time.

### Publishing your resource
After resource verification, we will **obfuscate your module for you** and provide you the files to upload to any [verified marketplace](/community/marketplaces/). This is to ensure
you publish the same version of the resource you just verified.

:::note
The publishing process may change before or after the release, but we will keep you updated.
:::
